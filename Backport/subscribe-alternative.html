Option 1: Quick Google Form (Recommended)

The easiest way to collect emails is using a Google Form. Here's how:

  1. Create a Google Form:
    • Go to forms.google.com
    • Click the "+" to create a new form
    • Title it "Slana Newsletter Signup"
    • Add one question: "Email Address" (make it required)
    • Click the palette icon to match your site colors
  2. Get the embed code:
    • Click "Send" button
    • Click the "<>" embed icon
    • Adjust width to 640 and height to 400
    • Copy the iframe code
  3. Add to your site:
